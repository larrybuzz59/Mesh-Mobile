What makes it different
Built for the real world,
not just the connected one.
📡
Dual-mode transport
Prefers Firebase over internet. Drops to Bluetooth LE + Wi-Fi Direct when offline. Switches automatically in under 500ms.
🔗
Store-and-forward mesh
Messages hop up to 7 devices to reach the recipient. Each device relays for others — no central tower needed.
💾
Local-first SQLite
Every message is persisted locally before any network attempt. The app works fully offline — sync happens when you reconnect.
🔁
Reconnect sync
Coming back online? MeshChat uploads your queued messages and downloads anything you missed in the right order.
🛡️
Deduplication
A message arriving via both mesh relay and internet sync at the same time appears exactly once. Rolling 60s dedup cache.
📶
Live mode indicator
Always-visible banner shows current mode, peer count, and relay path ("Relayed via 2 devices · 180ms").
Installation
Get it on your device
🍎 iOS — TestFlight
1
Install TestFlight from the App Store on your iPhone or iPad.
2
Tap the iOS button above (or scan the QR code) to open the TestFlight invite link.
3
Tap Accept in TestFlight, then Install. The app installs in seconds.
4
Allow Bluetooth when prompted — required for mesh mode to work.
🤖 Android — APK
1
Tap the Android button above (or scan the QR code) to download the APK.
2
If prompted, go to Settings → Install unknown apps and allow your browser.
3
Open the downloaded APK file and tap Install.
4
Allow Bluetooth and Nearby devices permissions when the app opens.
